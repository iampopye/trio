# Content and Messaging Strategy

Evaluate website content for marketing effectiveness, copy quality, and persuasion power. Score messaging across dimensions and provide actionable rewrite suggestions.

## When to Use

- Auditing website content and messaging quality
- Evaluating headline clarity, value propositions, and CTAs
- Scoring content across multiple pages
- Generating before/after rewrite examples

## Content Quality Scoring

Score each dimension 0-10:

### Headline Clarity (0-10)
- Does the homepage headline clearly communicate what the product/service does?
- Can a first-time visitor understand the value in under 5 seconds?
- Is it specific (not generic "We help businesses grow")?
- 9-10: crystal clear + compelling, 7-8: clear but generic, 5-6: somewhat unclear, 3-4: confusing, 0-2: no clear headline

### Value Proposition Strength (0-10)
- Is there a clear, differentiated value proposition?
- Does it answer "Why should I choose you over alternatives?"
- Is it specific with proof (numbers, outcomes, timeframes)?
- 9-10: unique + proven, 7-8: clear but unproven, 5-6: generic, 3-4: unclear, 0-2: missing

### Copy Persuasion (0-10)
- Does copy focus on benefits over features?
- Does it use customer language (not jargon)?
- Are there emotional triggers and logical proof?
- Does it address objections proactively?
- 9-10: highly persuasive + natural, 5-6: informational not persuasive, 0-2: poor

### Content Depth (0-10)
- Is there enough content to inform purchase decisions?
- Are features explained with context and outcomes?
- Is there educational content (blog, guides, resources)?

### Call-to-Action Effectiveness (0-10)
- Are CTAs clear, specific, and action-oriented?
- Do they use value-driven text?
- Are there CTAs at multiple points on the page?
- Is there a clear primary vs secondary CTA?

## Brand and Trust Assessment

### Brand Consistency (0-10)
- Visual consistency across pages
- Messaging consistency
- Professional design quality

### Trust Architecture (0-10)
- About page quality (team, story, mission)
- Contact information visibility
- Social proof placement and quality
- Privacy/security messaging
- Professional certifications

### Authority Signals (0-10)
- Thought leadership content
- Media mentions or press coverage
- Industry awards or recognition
- Community presence

## Growth Strategy Assessment

### Pricing Strategy (0-10)
- Transparent and easy to understand?
- Free tier, trial, or low-friction entry?
- Good-Better-Best structure?
- Upsell/expansion paths visible?

### Acquisition Channels (0-10)
- How many channels in use?
- Content marketing maturity
- SEO investment
- Social media presence
- Paid advertising indicators

### Retention & Expansion (0-10)
- Onboarding indicators
- Community/engagement features
- Upgrade paths
- Newsletter or ongoing communication
- Help center quality

## Analysis Output

For each page analyzed, document:
- **Wins** -- things done well (with specific quotes)
- **Fixes** -- improvements needed with specific rewrite suggestions
- **Missing** -- elements that should exist but don't

### Before/After Examples

For every significant issue, provide:
```
Before: "[current copy]"
After: "[improved copy]"
Why: [explanation of what changed and why it's better]
```

## Revenue Opportunity Identification

### Quick Wins (1-2 weeks)
- Pricing page optimizations
- CTA improvements
- Social proof additions
- Urgency elements

### Medium-Term (1-3 months)
- Content marketing expansion
- Email nurture sequences
- Competitive positioning pages
- Referral program

### Strategic (3-6 months)
- New acquisition channel development
- Product-led growth features
- Partnership strategy
- Community building

For each recommendation, estimate effort (Low/Medium/High), impact, timeline, and revenue effect.

## Key Principles

- Always fetch and read actual page content -- never guess
- Quote specific copy in the analysis
- Every fix must include a concrete alternative
- Score honestly -- do not inflate
- Focus on revenue impact -- prioritize issues affecting conversions
- Frame through a revenue lens, not just best practices
- Identify the single biggest growth lever
