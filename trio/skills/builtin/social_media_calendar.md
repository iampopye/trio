# Social Media Content Calendar

Generate complete 30-day content calendars with platform-specific posts, hooks, hashtags, and content repurposing strategies.

## When to Use

- Planning monthly social media content
- Creating platform-specific post copy
- Building content pillar strategies
- Developing hashtag and engagement tactics
- Repurposing long-form content across platforms

## Step 1: Platform Selection

| Platform | Best For | Content Type | Posting Frequency |
|----------|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, SaaS, professionals | Thought leadership, case studies | 3-5x/week |
| Twitter/X | Tech, media, creators | Hot takes, threads, engagement | 1-3x/day |
| Instagram | E-commerce, lifestyle, agencies | Carousels, Reels, Stories | 4-7x/week + daily Stories |
| TikTok | Consumer brands, education | Short-form video, trends | 1-3x/day |
| YouTube | Education, SaaS demos | Tutorials, reviews | 1-2x/week |
| Facebook | Local business, communities | Community, events, groups | 3-5x/week |

Select 2-3 primary platforms based on business type and audience.

## Step 2: Content Pillars

Define 4-5 pillars that anchor all content:

| Pillar | Type | Purpose | Mix |
|--------|------|---------|-----|
| 1 | Educational | Establish authority | 40% |
| 2 | Behind-the-Scenes | Build trust, humanize | 20% |
| 3 | Social Proof | Build credibility | 15% |
| 4 | Engagement | Build community | 15% |
| 5 | Promotional | Drive revenue | 10% |

## Step 3: Hook Formulas

### LinkedIn Hooks
```
"I [did/learned/lost] [specific thing] and here's what happened:"
"Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take about industry]"
"[Number] years in [industry]. Here's what nobody tells you:"
"Stop [common practice]. Start [better alternative]. Here's why:"
"The biggest mistake [audience] make with [topic]:"
```

### Twitter/X Hooks
```
"Here's a thread on [topic] nobody is talking about:"
"[Contrarian statement]. Let me explain."
"[Number] things I wish I knew about [topic]:"
"Hot take: [bold claim]"
```

### Instagram Hooks
```
"Save this for later"
"I tested [X] for [time]. Results inside."
"[Number] signs you're [problem] (and how to fix it)"
"My exact [framework/process] for [result]:"
```

### TikTok Hooks (First 3 Seconds)
```
"Wait, you're still doing [old way]?"
"Here's the [topic] hack nobody showed you"
"If you're a [audience], watch this"
"The #1 reason your [thing] isn't working"
```

## Step 4: Content Types by Platform

**LinkedIn:** Text posts 40%, Carousel documents 25%, Image + caption 15%, Video 10%, Polls 5%, Articles 5%

**Twitter/X:** Text tweets 40%, Threads 25%, Image + caption 15%, Quote tweets 10%, Polls 5%, Video 5%

**Instagram:** Carousels 35%, Reels 30%, Single image 15%, Stories 15%, Live 5%

**TikTok:** Trending formats 30%, Educational 30%, Behind-scenes 20%, Storytelling 15%, Duets 5%

## Step 5: Hashtag Strategy

| Tier | Posts Using Tag | Count Per Post | Purpose |
|------|----------------|----------------|---------|
| Niche | Under 100K | 3-5 | Targeted, easier to rank |
| Medium | 100K-1M | 3-5 | Moderate competition |
| Broad | 1M+ | 2-3 | Discovery potential |
| Branded | Custom | 1 | Brand recognition |

**Platform counts:** Instagram 5-15, LinkedIn 3-5, Twitter/X 1-2, TikTok 3-5

## Step 6: Engagement Tactics

**Questions (30% of posts):**
"What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?"
"Agree or disagree? [Statement]"

**Polls (1-2x per week):**
"What matters most to you in [category]?"

**Controversial/Debate (1-2x per week):**
"[Common advice] is terrible advice. Here's why..."

**Storytelling (1-2x per week):**
"3 years ago, I [starting point]. Today, [result]. Here's the journey:"

## Step 7: Repurposing Framework

Take ONE long-form piece and create 10+ posts:

```
SOURCE: 1 Blog Post / Podcast / Video / Newsletter

OUTPUT:
  1. LinkedIn text post -- key insight
  2. Twitter thread -- 5-7 takeaways
  3. Instagram carousel -- framework visualized
  4. Instagram Reel -- 30-second summary
  5. TikTok -- quick tip of #1 takeaway
  6. LinkedIn carousel -- PDF slideshow
  7. Twitter single tweet -- most quotable line
  8. Instagram Story -- behind-the-scenes
  9. YouTube Short -- condensed video
  10. Facebook post -- discussion question
```

**Schedule over 2 weeks:**
- Day 1: Publish original + share key insight
- Day 3: Carousel and Reel
- Day 5: TikTok and YouTube Short
- Day 7: Different angle or takeaway
- Day 10: Engagement question
- Day 14: Reshare with "in case you missed it"

## Step 8: 30-Day Calendar Structure

```
DAY 1 (Monday):
  LinkedIn: [Pillar 1 - Educational]
    Hook: "[text]"
    Post: [Full text, 150-300 words]
    Hashtags: #tag1 #tag2 #tag3
    Time: 9:00 AM

  Twitter/X: [Pillar 4 - Engagement]
    Tweet: "[text, under 280 chars]"
    Time: 12:00 PM

  Instagram: [Pillar 2 - Behind the Scenes]
    Caption: "[text, 100-200 words]"
    Visual: [description]
    Hashtags: [10-15]
    Time: 6:00 PM
    Type: Carousel (5 slides)
```

### Calendar Rules
- Each pillar appears at least 6 times per month
- Promotional content never appears 2 days in a row
- Engagement posts every 2-3 days
- Mix of content types per platform
- Flexible slots for trending content
